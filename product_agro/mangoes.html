Fresh Mangoes



Mango (Botanical Name: Mangifera indica) is the most important fruit of India and is known as “King of fruits”. India occupies top position among mango growing countries of the world and produces 40.48% of the total world mango production.The fruit is cultivated in the largest area i.e 2,312 thousand ha and the production is around 15.03 million tons

RGS International offers the very popular variety of mango in India which is Alphonso & Kesar

Alphonso - This variety is medium in size, ovate oblique in shape and orange yellow in color. The pulp is yellow to orange in color. It is soft, firm and fibreless. It is a mid-season variety.

Kesar - Fruits are medium sized, flesh is sweet and fibreless. Color is apricot yellow with red blush, an early season variety.

Each individual fruit of mango is covered in a clean, white, soft, expandable and netted type polystyrene sleeve to prevent bruising before packing in a box.

Mangoes are packed in well ventilated insect-proof boxes with printing or labeling done with non-toxic ink or glue.

Mangoes are spacked in each container in compliance with the Recommended International Code of Practice for Packaging and Transport of Fresh Fruits and Vegetables.
